Privacy Policy - MedTimers

Effective Date: 3 April 2026

MedTimers ("we", "our", or "the app") is designed to help users track medication timing safely and reliably. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data Storage

MedTimers stores all data locally on your device.

This includes:

  • Timer names
  • Dose timestamps
  • Medication intervals and settings

We do not collect, transmit, or store this data on external servers.

2. Personal Data

MedTimers does not collect any personally identifiable information.

No account is required to use the app.

3. Permissions

MedTimers may request the following permissions:

Notifications
Used to alert you when it is safe to administer the next dose.

Exact Alarm Permission (Android)
Used to ensure notifications are delivered at the correct time without delay.

These permissions are used solely for core app functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data.

5. Third-Party Services

MedTimers does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
